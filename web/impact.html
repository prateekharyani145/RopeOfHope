Impact on Students

Impact upon students is the justification for existence of Rope Of Hope. Those students who are described as children with no potential or lack the hope for a better future are undertaken to spur curiosity and creativity.

Primarily, students concentrate on quality learning of foundation subjects which allows them to build greater self-esteem and establish the importance of well-being. Students are provided fairness in coverage of the school curriculum that includes development of various technical and social skills. They develop a perception of their society and living conditions which ultimately stimulates the need for change. In the process students acquire socio-economic values and human ethics such as relationship building, problem solving, self-awareness, strategic planning with limited resources, team building and accountability. The experience of rising from an under privileged lifestyle teaches them desire and will to facilitate equality and fairness for themselves and others.

... With our teaching techniques inspired by different practices of various cultures and customs such as Gurukuls, students develop abstract thinking, by making live model and performing practicals they get a better understanding of the subject. It allows them to keep a clear vision regarding their future in respect with further studies.

Students get a platform to make something meaningful out of their talents and potential. Since, Rope Of Hope is working with the age group from where one begins to identify themselves and look for a place to fit in the society, children get to explore various opportunities. They meet people with similar mindlike which allows them to unleash their true potential and feel appreciated. We give Attention to various fields inclusive of photography, modern agricultural, craft, dance, music, rap, innovations with existing tools, creative writing, yoga, environmental awareness, and more to be added.

Extensively, they receive proper care for hygiene and health. Medical check ups are organised regularly. This prevents them from indulging into inappropriate practices and maintain well-being. It is also supported by regular discussions by our team with parents.



Impact on Volunteer

"You get what you give and also what you're willing to take."

Rope Of Hope is built upon five core values, fidelity,uniqueness, Insightful, kindness, and Hope, all of which we share with our team.You will inculcate our core values leading to the development of your character.

Being a skill-based volunteer organisation this opportunity will allow you to make meaningful contributions with your existing skill set and help to refine them.You will learn ground realities and gain experience in real-time environment of working for a social cause. It will sharpen interpersonal and intrapersonal skills, communication skills, problem solving, multitasking, innovation and creativity

... It is an opportunity to be surrounded by people who inspire and motivate you and allow networking with a similar mind like people. Which will provide you access to a wider world and help to form a clearer vision for your future in this field.

Working in an NGO will instil a sense of selflessness and altruism. Since we impact the lives of students and help them rise from an under privileged lifestyle teaching them desire and will to facilitate equality and fairness for themselves and others, we ultimately make a lasting impact on the lives of others and yours.

Certificate of NGOs have credibility and add value to your resume and speak volumes about your character as in the process it helps you to become a better version of yourself.
