Mission

To provide a Creative and joyous platform for children to explore their unique talents.

Vision

Children and Volunteers unleashing their uniquness and talents.

Our Activity

Daily Classes

Individuals, driven by enthusiasm to transform the sketch of a usual classroom into a uniquely designed platform for children to learn foundation subjects serve as teachers to students of a government school. Volunteers teach hindi, English and basic mathematics concentrating on insightful and practical learning.

Remedial Sunday

Sundays are dedicated to the children of Slums in Bhawarkua, Indore. We provide remedial classes to these students for foundation subjects that are English, Hindi, and basic Mathematics. Compassionate individuals dedicate the time to under-resourced students and provide a sense of direction with basic education.

Creative Saturday

Among the hustle of learning and teaching the foundation subjects, Rope of Hope entitles students to dedicate a day to artful activities such as art and craft, photography, music, dance or outdoor activities inclusive of sports. These Saturdays work as mind boosters that ultimately help a child to learn and retain better.

Mission Dream School

It is an initiative to change classrooms into functional spaces and take teaching methods beyond chalk-and-talk in most under-resourced schools with Eco-friendly and sustainable approaches. Additional emphasis is established on hygiene.
“If they do not have a textbook, then let there not be a need for it.”

Mission Smile

“A smile is the most necessary thing a child should wear.”

Rope Of Hope celebrates children’s day with hundreds of students.The First 'mission smile' was accomplished on 14th November 2018 with more than a thousand slum students. In 2019 students from 5 official government schools joined us.
